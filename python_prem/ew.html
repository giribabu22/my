Introduction

IIT Delhi is one of the most prestigious public technical universities in India. It was established in the year 1961 as the College of Engineering. Later declared an Institution of National Importance and renamed as Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. It is the dream college of millions of students. Are you the one?


To study in this top-class institution, you have to follow certain instructions, fulfil the eligibility criterion, clear JEE Main and Advanced exams, and many more. Here, you will find all the details related to how to get admission to IIT Delhi and what are the cut-offs for Delhi 2022. But, before getting into deep, let’s just get a brief overview of IIT Delhi, and then we will answer all your questions.

Admission Process in IIT Delhi

The admission process in IIT Delhi follows a systematic approach and maintains different timelines for different courses. It helps students or parents to not get confused and easily understand the flow of communication and the admissions process.


Initially, the National Testing Agency (NTA) will conduct the JEE Examinations. The students who qualify for the exam will get a call for counselling. Counselling will be done in many phases according to the merit list announced by NTA. During the counselling, candidates can select IIT Delhi as their preference. Candidates who have the required cut-off will get admission to IIT Delhi.

Scholarships

IIT Delhi offers the scholarship to meritorious students who are eligible for Merit-cum-means (MCM) scholarships. For the MCM scholarship, the family income of the candidate should be less than 4.5 LPA and the candidate must score 6 CGPA. But, an utmost 25% of students can get this scholarship that provides a tuition waiver and an amount of 1000 Rs. And, only 10% of students provided only tuition waivers.


SC/ST candidates will get full relaxation from the tuition fee.


Other notable alumni and industrialists provide scholarships to the students based on their family income and academic performance. A list of some other scholarships is given below:

Type of the Scholarship

Details

The student with the lowest means will be awarded this scholarship. The student eligible for this scholarship shall not be a recipient of any other scholarship.

One scholarship is awarded to 2nd-year B.Tech students of Textile Technology based on MCM having a CGPA of 6.0 or more.

Bina Dewan Singh Scholarship

One scholarship is awarded every year to a 1st-year student of the undergraduate programs based on MCM having a family income less than or equal to Rs. 6.0 LPA.

Gobind H. Keswani Scholarship

One MCM scholarship will be provided every year to a first-year student of the UG program admitted through IIT JEE having a family income not exceeding Rs. 5 LPA. Renewal subject to maintaining a GPA of 7.0

CS&E 1998 Batch Scholarship

This is for computer engineering students. One scholarship is awarded to a 2nd-year student from a Computer Science & Engineering course based on MCM. The student shall not be a recipient of any other scholarship.

Placements

Most of the MNCs visit every year to IIT Delhi. Usually, 80% of the students will step out from this institute with at least one offer letter in hand. The remaining 20% of students opt for options like further studies, preparation for civil services, start-ups, and research work and do not take part in the recruitment process. That's the reason many people wish to study at the Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi. Among all the companies, Microsoft is one of the leading recruiters from IIT Delhi.

FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

1. Does IIT Delhi Offers Some Facilities in Terms of Placements?

It's of course. IIT Delhi always strives to give its best to its students in all aspects. They provide guidance, tips, and facilities in their placements also. The institute has a separate placement to sell. Also all the faculty concentrate on teaching some general concepts like:

  • Communication skills

  • Preparation of curriculum vitae

  • Mock interviews

  • Jam sessions, group discussions

  • Presentation skills

  • Case studies

  • Problem-solving techniques etc.

2. Which Sector has More Chances of Placements?

Though all sectors have equal importance and opportunities, comparatively the core sectors may have more placement chances than other IT courses.

3. For Which Courses, the IIT Delhi was Famous?

The IIT Delhi is famous for its integrated courses, dual programs. Computer engineering, mathematics, computing, electrical engineering, etc. were very famous, and the students got sound knowledge.

4. How Many Hostels are There in IIT Delhi?

The IIT Delhi campus has 13 hostels all together. Separate Hostels for girls and boys. But each hostel has students from all years and all groups.

5. How will be the Allocation Process of Hostels in IIT Delhi?

The allocation of hostels on the serial number of the admission process. It is simple and straightforward. Every allegation of seats and hostels will be transparent to everyone and done in a structured way.

6. How can I get admission to IIT Delhi 2022?

To get admission to the IIT Delhi UG programme, you have to clear the JEE Main and JEE Advanced Exam. Once you clear the exam, you will get a call for counselling. Here, you need to fill the preference institute, add IIT Delhi, and select the branch. If you have above or equal cut-off marks as required for admission, you will get admission to the IIT Delhi.

7. What are the eligibility criteria to get admission to IIT Delhi?

The candidates should pass Class 12 in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics stream (PCM) with 75% aggregate (65% for SC/ ST candidates) along with English as a compulsory subject. There is no age limit for the candidate to appear in this exam. But, the candidates who appeared in +2 in 2021 or appearing in 2022 are only eligible to apply for this exam. Also, a good rank in JEE Advanced is necessary to be eligible for B.Tech admissions.
